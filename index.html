Teacher Bullying

How to stop teacher bullying

1: Stand Up for Teachers: If you witness bullying, speak up in a respectful way. Letting the bully know that their behavior is unacceptable. This can make a difference.

2: Be a Positive Role Model: Set an example by treating teachers and fellow students with respect. Your actions can inspire others to do the same.

3: Build Friendships: Build positive relationships with teachers. A supportive student-teacher dynamic can help deter bullying behaviors.
